Our Story

A Legacy of Strong Foundations

What started as a small, family-owned contractor has grown into one of the region's leading construction firms. Stackly was founded on the belief that a building is only as good as the integrity of the people building it.

For over two decades, we have partnered with communities, businesses, and families to bring their architectural dreams to life with uncompromising quality.
